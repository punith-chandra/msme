🚀 Coir Vikas Yojana (CVY)

Empowering Entrepreneurs • Generating Employment • Building India

🎯 Objective

Coir Vikas Yojana (CVY) is an umbrella scheme implemented by the Coir Board for the development of the coir industry throughout India.

  • Increase income of workers, entrepreneurs and exporters.
  • Promote utilization of coir resources available in India.
  • Develop improved machinery, processes and products.
  • Promote investment in the coir industry.
  • Generate employment opportunities.
  • Empower rural women.
  • Provide welfare measures for coir workers.
  • Modernize the coir sector through technology and digital platforms.

⚙️ Major Components

  • Science & Technology – Research, modernization and technology upgradation.
  • Skill Upgradation – Training programmes for workers and entrepreneurs.
  • Mahila Coir Yojana – Women-focused self-employment programme with training and stipend.
  • Export Market Promotion – Participation in international exhibitions and export support.
  • Domestic Market Promotion – Sales depots, exhibitions and marketing assistance.
  • Trade & Industry Support Services – Market studies, data analysis and industry reports.
  • Welfare Measures – Welfare schemes for coir workers.

✅ Benefits

  • Training with stipend support.
  • Skill development for entrepreneurs and workers.
  • Employment generation opportunities.
  • Marketing assistance in India and abroad.
  • Technology upgradation support.
  • Support for rural women artisans.
  • Research and innovation support.

👤 Eligibility

All Coir Production and Processing Units registered with the Coir Board and possessing a valid Udyam Registration Certificate.

📄 Documents Required

  • Aadhaar Card
  • PAN Card
  • Business Registration Certificate
  • Project Report
  • Bank Account Details
  • Photographs

📝 Application Process

  1. Apply through Coir Board.
  2. Submit project proposal.
  3. Upload required documents.
  4. Verification by authorities.
  5. Approval and release of assistance.

🏦 Financial Assistance

  • Support for coir industry modernization.
  • Skill development assistance.
  • Market promotion support.
