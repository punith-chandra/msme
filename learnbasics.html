About PMEGP

PMEGP is a flagship MSME scheme that provides financial assistance for establishing new micro-enterprises in manufacturing and service sectors.

Benefits

Subsidy Support

Up to 35% subsidy for eligible applicants.

Bank Loan

Easy access to business loans.

Employment

Generate self-employment opportunities.

Business Growth

Support for startups and entrepreneurs.

Eligibility

✔ Age above 18 years
✔ New business ventures only
✔ Minimum 8th pass for projects above specified limits
