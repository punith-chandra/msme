🚀 Prime Minister's Employment Generation Programme (PMEGP)

Empowering Entrepreneurs • Generating Employment • Building India

🎯 Objective

Prime Minister's Employment Generation Programme (PMEGP) is a credit-linked subsidy scheme launched by the Ministry of MSME. The main objective is to generate self-employment opportunities through the establishment of micro-enterprises in rural and urban areas.

✅ Benefits

  • Financial assistance for setting up new enterprises.
  • Subsidy up to 35% of project cost for eligible beneficiaries.
  • Employment generation in rural and urban regions.
  • Support for manufacturing and service sector projects.
  • Encourages entrepreneurship among youth.

👤 Eligibility

  • Minimum age: 18 years.
  • Individuals, Self Help Groups (SHGs), Trusts and Societies.
  • Educational qualification of at least VIII standard for projects above ₹10 lakh (manufacturing) and ₹5 lakh (service).
  • Only new projects are eligible.

📄 Documents Required

  • Aadhaar Card
  • PAN Card
  • Passport-size Photograph
  • Project Report
  • Educational Qualification Certificates
  • Bank Account Details

📝 Application Process

  1. Visit the PMEGP official portal.
  2. Register as an applicant.
  3. Fill the online application form.
  4. Upload required documents.
  5. Submit the application.
  6. Track application status online.

💰 Project Cost Limits

  • Manufacturing Sector: Up to ₹50 Lakhs
  • Service Sector: Up to ₹20 Lakhs
