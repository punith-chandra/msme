🚀 Promotion of MSMEs in NER & Sikkim

Empowering Entrepreneurs • Generating Employment • Building India

🎯 Objective

To encourage industrial growth and entrepreneurship in North Eastern Region and Sikkim.

✅ Benefits

  • Infrastructure development.
  • Technology support.
  • Skill development.
  • Entrepreneurship promotion.

👤 Eligibility

MSMEs located in North Eastern States and Sikkim.

📄 Documents Required

  • Aadhaar Card
  • PAN Card
  • Udyam Registration
  • Project Report
  • Bank Account Details

📝 Application Process

  1. Submit project proposal.
  2. Upload required documents.
  3. Verification by MSME authorities.
  4. Approval of project.
  5. Release of financial assistance.

🏦 Financial Assistance

  • Infrastructure development support.
  • Industrial growth assistance.
  • Special support for NER & Sikkim MSMEs.
