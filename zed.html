PMEGP Scheme

🚀 MSME Sustainable ZED Certification Scheme

Empowering Entrepreneurs • Generating Employment • Building India

🎯 Objective

The ZED (Zero Defect Zero Effect) Scheme promotes quality manufacturing, environmental sustainability, and global competitiveness among MSMEs.

✅ Benefits

  • ZED Certification for MSMEs.
  • Quality improvement support.
  • Environmental sustainability promotion.
  • Enhanced global competitiveness.
  • Subsidy on certification costs.

👤 Eligibility

  • All registered MSMEs.
  • Manufacturing sector enterprises.
  • MSMEs willing to undergo assessment.

📄 Documents Required

  • Udyam Registration Certificate.
  • PAN Card.
  • GST Registration.
  • Business Profile.
  • Manufacturing Process Details.
  • Quality Management Documents.

📝 Application Process

  1. Register on ZED Portal.
  2. Complete self-assessment.
  3. Submit required information.
  4. Third-party assessment.
  5. Certification approval.
  6. Receive ZED rating and benefits.

🏅 Certification Support

  • Bronze, Silver, Gold and Diamond certification levels.
  • Financial assistance for assessment.
  • Support for quality improvement initiatives.
