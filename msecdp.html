🚀 Micro & Small Enterprises Cluster Development Programme (MSE-CDP)

Empowering Entrepreneurs • Generating Employment • Building India

🎯 Objective

The MSE-CDP scheme aims to enhance the productivity, competitiveness, and capacity of Micro and Small Enterprises through cluster-based development and infrastructure support.

✅ Benefits

  • Common Facility Centers (CFCs) for MSMEs.
  • Infrastructure development support.
  • Technology upgradation assistance.
  • Improved competitiveness of MSME clusters.
  • Better market access and productivity.

👤 Eligibility

  • Existing MSME clusters.
  • State Governments and Implementing Agencies.
  • Special Purpose Vehicles (SPVs).
  • Industry Associations and Cooperatives.

📄 Documents Required

  • Detailed Project Report (DPR).
  • Cluster Profile Report.
  • SPV Registration Documents.
  • Land Ownership/Lease Documents.
  • Financial Statements.
  • Bank Details.

📝 Application Process

  1. Identify eligible cluster.
  2. Prepare Detailed Project Report.
  3. Submit proposal through State Government or Implementing Agency.
  4. Technical appraisal and approval.
  5. Release of funds.
  6. Project implementation and monitoring.

💰 Project Cost Limits

  • Common Facility Centre projects receive assistance as per scheme guidelines.
  • Infrastructure Development projects receive central grant support.
  • Funding varies according to project category and approval.
