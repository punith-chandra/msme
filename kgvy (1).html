🚀 Khadi Gramodyog Vikas Yojana (KGVY)

Empowering Entrepreneurs • Generating Employment • Building India

🎯 Objective

To strengthen Khadi and Village Industries through modernization and infrastructure development.

✅ Benefits

  • Infrastructure support.
  • Marketing assistance.
  • Technology upgradation.
  • Employment generation.

👤 Eligibility

Khadi Institutions and Village Industry Units approved by KVIC.

📄 Documents Required

  • Aadhaar Card
  • PAN Card
  • Khadi Institution Registration
  • Bank Details
  • Project Proposal

📝 Application Process

  1. Apply through KVIC.
  2. Submit project proposal.
  3. Upload documents.
  4. Verification by KVIC.
  5. Approval and release of assistance.

🏦 Financial Assistance

  • Khadi infrastructure support.
  • Marketing assistance.
  • Production enhancement support.
