🚀 ASPIRE Scheme

Empowering Entrepreneurs • Generating Employment • Building India

🎯 Objective

A Scheme for Promotion of Innovation, Rural Industries and Entrepreneurship.

✅ Benefits

  • Support for startups.
  • Livelihood business incubators.
  • Technology business incubators.
  • Innovation promotion.

👤 Eligibility

Entrepreneurs, startups and innovation-focused organizations.

📄 Documents Required

  • Aadhaar Card of applicant/organization.
  • PAN Card.
  • Detailed Project Report (DPR).
  • Proof of Institution/Organization Registration.
  • Bank Account Details.
  • Land/Building Ownership or Lease Documents.
  • Financial Statements (if applicable).
  • Other documents as prescribed by MSME Ministry.

📝 Application Process

  1. Identify the appropriate ASPIRE component (LBI, TBI, or Livelihood Business Incubator).
  2. Prepare a Detailed Project Report.
  3. Submit proposal to the Ministry of MSME or designated implementing agency.
  4. Provide required supporting documents.
  5. Proposal is evaluated by the competent authority.
  6. After approval, financial assistance is released as per scheme guidelines.
  7. Implement the project and submit progress reports periodically.

🏦 Financial Assistance / Project Support

  • Support for setting up Livelihood Business Incubators (LBIs).
  • Support for Technology Business Incubators (TBIs).
  • Funding assistance for innovation and entrepreneurship development.
  • Support for skill development and incubation activities.
  • Financial assistance varies depending on the type of project and approved component.
